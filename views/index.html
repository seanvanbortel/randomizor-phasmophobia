Phasmophobia Randomizer

This randomizer I've build is made to choose a map, light source and equipment to get a more challenging game in Phasmophobia. The playstyle is up to you, but I recommend to select one map (obviously), one light source and three things from the equipment. It's quiet simple, just click each link which leads you to a randomizer and most important, have fun playing. Good luck!
